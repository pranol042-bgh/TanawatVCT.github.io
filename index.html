About

Dr. Tanawat Fongsri is an orthopaedic surgeon specialising in Sports Medicine at Bangkok Hospital Phuket. He is bilingual in Thai and English and serves on the hospital's management team as Deputy/Assistant Hospital Director. Bangkok Hospital Phuket (BPK) is part of BDMS, Thailand’s largest private hospital network.

Clinical Services & Interests

    Arthroscopic and sports-injury care, knee and shoulder preservation, and return-to-sport planning.

  • General orthopaedics & fracture care
